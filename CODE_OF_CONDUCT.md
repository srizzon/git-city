# English

# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior:**

- Being respectful and inclusive
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what's best for the community
- Showing empathy towards others

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainer at **samuel@thegitcity.com** (or via DM on [X/Twitter](https://x.com/samuelrizzondev)).

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated. The project team is obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter.

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org), version 2.1.

# 中文

# 行为准则

## 我们的承诺

作为社区成员、贡献者和负责人，我们承诺：无论年龄、体型、显性或隐性残疾、种族、生理特征、性别认同与表达、经验水平、教育背景、社会经济地位、国籍、个人外貌、人种、宗教信仰、性取向如何，都要让所有人在参与本社区时，享有无骚扰的体验。

## 行为规范

### 积极行为：
- 保持尊重与包容的态度
- 主动给出并虚心接受建设性反馈
- 聚焦于对社区最有利的方向
- 对他人展现同理心

### 不可接受的行为：
- 恶意挑衅、发表侮辱/贬损性言论、进行人身攻击或政治攻击
- 公开或私下的骚扰行为
- 未经明确许可，公开他人隐私信息
- 其他可被合理认定为不当的行为

## 执行机制

若发现辱骂、骚扰或其他不可接受的行为，可通过邮箱 **samuel@thegitcity.com**（或通过 [X/Twitter](https://x.com/samuelrizzondev) 私信）向项目维护者举报。

所有投诉都会被审核和调查，项目团队有义务对举报人的信息严格保密。

## 协议出处

本行为准则改编自《贡献者公约》（[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org)）2.1 版本。

### 总结
1. 行为准则核心是保障社区无骚扰、包容的参与环境，明确区分了积极行为和不可接受行为的边界；
2. 举报不当行为可通过指定邮箱或社交平台私信，项目方会保密并核查所有投诉；
3. 本准则基于《贡献者公约》2.1版改编，具备行业通用的合规性。
